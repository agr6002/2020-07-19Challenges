Challenge 6

Given an integer array nums, find the contiguous subarray within an array (containing at least one number) which has the largest product.

Example 1:

Input: [2,3,-2,4]

Output: 6

Why? [2,3] has the largest product 6.

Example 2:

Input: [-2,0,-1]

Output: 0

Why? The result cannot be 2, because [-2,-1] is not a subarray.
